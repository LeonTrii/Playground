Masahiro Sakurai

A very epic man

My thoughts about this amazing man:

Masahiro Sakurai is an inspirational model. He takes on numerous challenges which make Super Smash Bros. an incredibly unique fighting game. Truely a man of determination.

A short timeline

  • 1970: Born on August 3rd, in Musahimurayama, Toyko.
  • 1989: Worked for HAL Laboratory. Created Kirby and conducted Kirby's Dream Land at 19.
  • 2003: Left HAL Laboratory on August 5th. Got exhausted of the sequalization stress.
  • 2005: Worked on a project with Q Entertainment and Tetsuya Mizuguchi. Thus, the game Meteos, was created. On September 30th, he founded a new company, Sora.
  • 2008: Provided the voice for King Dedede in Kirby 64: The Crystal Shards. Nintendo and Sora started a first-party studio called Project Sora.
  • 2012: Project Sora worked on Kid Icarus: Uprising. Project Sora ended on June 20th.
  • 2013: Near his right shoulder, he got diagnosed with calcific tendinitis. He said that it could slow down his work.
  • 2015: Considered retirement expressing his concerns that he wouldn't be able to make more games in his stressful state. He did not think that there would be another game for Super Smash Bros.
