Chemistry Conversions

A web application to help with UW CHEM 110

The conversions are not 100 percent accurate, I made this to check my work more effciently

Temperature Conversions

The temperature is rounded to the tenths place

==============================================

Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit

(°C × 9/5) + 32 = °F

==============================================

Convert Fahrenheit to Celsius

(°F − 32) × 5/9 = °C

==============================================

Convert Celsius to Kelvins

°C + 273 = K

==============================================

Convert Kelvins to Celsius

K − 273 = °C

==============================================

Convert Fahrenheit to Kelvins

(°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273 = K

==============================================

Convert Kelvins to Fahrenheit

(K − 273) × 9/5 + 32 = °F

==============================================
