About

Have you ever wanted to desperately fill a fridge? Maybe you would like to put your fridge filling skills to the test. Perhaps you are broke or maybe your fridge is too full to fill up. Fill Up the Fridge is a relaxing, popular simulation RPG. You can fill the fridge with merely a computer!

With five challenging levels and numerous food items, you will be filling fridges for hours. The game is open source; hopefully you know how to use a code editor or IDE if you want to try it. It costs a whopping $99.99 for the trial version. Or, you can just get the source code here if you're a nerd. If you beat the world record, you will receive a $100,000 cash prize (not guaranteed).
